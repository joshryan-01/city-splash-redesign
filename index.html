01 — Who we are

One day a year, South London becomes the capital of the culture.

City Splash is a 30,000-capacity festival that celebrates Caribbean and African culture — a space for the community to unite, connect and create the future. Every May, Brockwell Park fills with reggae, dancehall, jungle, garage, afrobeats and amapiano, brought by the heritage acts who built this culture and the new generation carrying it forward.

Six stages. Sound system royalty. The best jerk chicken you'll eat all year. This is the home of culture — come find your people.
