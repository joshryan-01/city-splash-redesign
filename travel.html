Travel & Sustainability

How to get to Brockwell Park, and how we're working to leave it better than we found it.

Travel

Getting to Brockwell Park

Brockwell Park sits between Herne Hill and Brixton in South London. We strongly encourage travelling by public transport. There is no general public parking on site.

  • Rail: Herne Hill station is the closest, a short walk from the park.
  • Tube: Brixton (Victoria line) is roughly a 15–20 minute walk.
  • Bus: Multiple routes stop along Norwood Road and Brixton Water Lane.

Sustainability

Leaving the park better than we found it

We're working towards a lower-impact festival: reusable cup schemes, on-site recycling and waste sorting, and supporting the park's post-event recovery so Brockwell Park stays healthy for the community that uses it year-round.
