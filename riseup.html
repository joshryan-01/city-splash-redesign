Talent development

Rise Up

Championing Caribbean talent for the global stage.

Since 2021

A platform built for the next generation

Rise Up is City Splash's dynamic talent development platform, supporting outstanding emerging artists from the Caribbean. Now in its fourth year, the programme gives selected artists a one-week creative residency built around the festival itself.

Residency artists get festival performance slots, songwriter camps, media exposure, industry event access, professional development workshops, and documentary coverage of their journey.
