Community

More Than a Festival

City Splash exists because of the community that built it.

Our commitment

Minimising disruption, maximising opportunity

City Splash is committed to making sure that event management plans minimise disruption to park users and the local community. The organisers are also actively looking to engage residents with the event and create opportunities for performers, traders and workers from the local area to be involved, as well as ongoing investment into a local community fund.

A movement

More than a festival: a movement

City Splash exists because of the community that built it. Every year we work with local artists, traders and grassroots organisations across South London, and we back causes that matter to our community when it counts.
