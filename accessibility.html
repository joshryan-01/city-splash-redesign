Access at City Splash

Accessibility

We want everyone to enjoy City Splash. Here's what's in place, and how to arrange what you need ahead of the day.

Book ahead

Bookable facilities

  • Personal Assistant tickets — complimentary for eligible attendees with the +1 symbol on their access card. Apply at least two weeks before the event.
  • Accessible viewing platforms — raised platforms at four stages with seating, charging points and accessible restrooms. First-come, first-served.
  • Accessible parking — a limited number of Blue Badge spaces. First-come, first-served.

On the day

Available without booking

  • Access hub with support staff and restrooms
  • Accessible toilets throughout the site
  • BSL interpretation for main stage performances
  • Dedicated access entry lanes at all gates
  • Assistance dog access with a designated area
  • Charging stations for mobility equipment
  • Medication refrigeration and a Changing Places facility
  • Hearing loops at multiple locations
  • Large-print menus and lowered bar counters

Good to know

The site itself

Brockwell Park is predominantly grass with limited hard-standing pathways, so the ground can get muddy in wet weather. Expect some inclines, with temporary plastic matting laid at stages.

Get in touch

Questions about access?

Email us and we'll aim to respond within 5 working days.
